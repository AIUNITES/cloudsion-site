❓ Quick Help

What's a Personal Access Token?

A PAT lets apps access your GitHub safely. Create one with "repo" scope to deploy sites.

Is this free?

Yes! GitHub Pages hosting is 100% free. Unlimited sites, no monthly fees.

Is my token safe?

Yes - stored only in your browser, never sent to our servers. Revoke anytime on GitHub.

What files do I need?

At minimum an index.html. You can also include CSS, JS, and images.
